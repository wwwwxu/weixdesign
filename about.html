Design, for me, is a process of continually approaching the truth.

My design journey began at Dalian University of Technology, where I studied disciplines like product design and visual communication. During my academic journey, I discovered my passion and aptitude for finding solutions to problems and designing with empathy for users. This led me to pursue a master's degree in design at Arizona State University.

After completing my degree, I embarked on a three-month internship at the loe design studio, applying design research methods for preliminary research on Midea Group's products. Following the internship, I joined Hesai Technology as an interaction designer, gaining extensive experience, particularly in user experience and practical B2B software. During my time at the company, I honed my skills in interdisciplinary communication and project management.

Since leaving the company, I have been self-studying front-end development, preparing for a broader and deeper career path in the future.
